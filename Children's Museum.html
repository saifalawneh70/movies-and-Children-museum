The Children’s Museum is NOW OPEN

Same museum you love. New ways to keep you safe.

In collaboration with an advisory council of scientists, physicians, and public health officials we’ve implemented these procedures and more to help ensure a safe and extraordinary experience for your family. Advance tickets are required for all visitors, including members and donors.

  • Daily capacity is limited to 38% of museum's pre-COVID capacity.
  • Everyone is expected to follow social distancing requirements.
  • Face masks are required for visitors 2 years old and older (unless medically unable to wear a mask).
  • PlayscapeⓇ and The Children’s Museum Guild’s Fantasy Tree House of Sport are closed. See all exhibit updates here
  • If you are not feeling well, please stay home. We will gladly exchange your ticket for a day when you feel better!
