BIRTHDAY EXPERIENCES

EXPERIENCE YOUR BIRTHDAY AT THE MUSEUM!

IT’S A PIECE OF CAKE!

At DISCOVERY Children’s Museum, we make birthday celebrations extra special for the honoree…and extra easy for the host. Every Birthday Experience includes all-access to our nine galleries packed with extraordinary hands-on adventures that focus on science, nature, art and early childhood education.

BIRTHDAY EXPERIENCE PACKAGES INCLUDE:

  • Unlimited use of the museum on the day of your Birthday Experience (before or after the celebration, during business hours)
  • 90 minutes of festivities in a party room
  • Set-up and clean up assistance provided by the museum
  • 30-minute Birthday Experience activity
  • Birthday cake from Freed’s Bakery (1/4 sheet cake) with candles, ice cream cups, and drink
  • Party favors: Favor Bags (ages 5 and up) or Musical Toy (ages 4 and under)
  • Streaming Music
  • Customized video slideshow of birthday boy or girl
  • Place setting in color of your choice
  • Option of birthday gift registry through the DISCOVERY retail store

AVAILABILITY

Birthday Experiences are available during regular museum operating hours on Saturday and Sunday only. VIP Birthday Experience available Sunday only. Dates and times are subject to availability; please reserve your Birthday Experience 3 weeks in advance of the requested date.

PRICING

Prices start at $395 for a group of up to 32 people. A 50% non-refundable credit card deposit is required. Special discounts are available for museum members. Birthday Experience prices and programming are subject to change.

ADD-ONS

  • $15 per each additional guest
  • Full catering from Broadway Pizzeria
  • Custom cake
  • Room upgrade
  • Extra time in room
