MEMBERSHIP GUIDELINES:
  1. DISCOVERY Children’s Museum memberships are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  2. Memberships and their privileges cannot be used in conjunction with group visits, e.g., school fieldtrips, birthday parties, etc.
  3. The museum issues announcements and membership information via email blasts. Members are encouraged to keep their email and postal addresses up-to-date by notifying the Membership Office of any changes in their contact information.
  4. Adults who are at least 18 years of age can be cardholding members. Children under age 18 years are considered guests of the cardholding member.
  5. Children under the age of 13 years must be supervised by an adult at all times while in the museum.
  6. Memberships are only valid to admit up to the total number specific to a membership level once a day.
  7. Members are not allowed to randomly invite general admission visitors to join their group for free admission to the museum.
  8. At least one (1) cardholder must be present and show valid government issued photo identification for admission of member guests to the museum. For “Member Express Check-In” you must show your membership card and valid government issued photo identification.
  9. Only the primary cardholder may make changes to a membership.
  10. Members or guests of a member who conduct themselves in a manner deemed discourteous to other museum guests or staff will be asked to leave the museum property.
  11. DISCOVERY Children’s Museum reserves the right to revoke a membership at any time for any reason.
